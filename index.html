SheHacks

SheHacks is IIITA's premier women-only tech event, where women coding enthusiasts converge for three days of tech talks, workshops, and an exhilarating 36-hour hackathon. Designed to foster learning, collaboration, and innovation, SheHacks offers aspiring female developers a platform to showcase their skills, tackle real-world challenges, and connect with like-minded individuals. Join us in this inclusive and empowering environment as we hack our way towards a more inclusive and digitally empowered future.

Venue : CC3 IIITA
Time : 8:30pm
Date : 8-10 Apr
